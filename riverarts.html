The researcher chose a survey research design because it best served to answerthe questions and the purposes of the study. The survey research is one in which a group of people or items is studied bycollecting and analyzing data from only a few people or items considered to be representative of the entire group. In other words, only a part of the population is studied, and findings from this are expected to be generalized to the entire population (Nworgu 1991:68). Similary, McBurney (1994:170) defines the survey assessing public opinion or individual characteristics by the use of questionnaire and sampling methods.
